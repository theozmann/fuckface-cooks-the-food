The Dinner Zimmer Salad

Welcome to the wonderful world of Andrew Panton

In efforts to honor the legendary man Don Zimmer himself, Andrew decided to research all locations where Zimmer has worked. Andrew then took ingredients than can be locally sourced from those locations to concoct the amazing Dinner Zimmer salad. What? It doesn't sound very good? Well go fuck yourself, the Vancouver Child Kicker himself hand crafted this beauty, so it's good.
